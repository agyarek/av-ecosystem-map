How to read this map

Every autonomous ride is a loop

Four stages repeat, thousands of times a day: a request comes in, a driver-that-is-software takes it, a purpose-built vehicle carries it, and a pit crew turns the vehicle around for the next one. Eleven industry layers slot into these four stages — tap any layer to jump to its companies, or press RUN LOOP and follow one ride around the circuit.

Beyond this map

Adjacent worlds of autonomy

The passenger loop above is one world of autonomy among several. Freight trucking and delivery robotics share some of its suppliers, but they run on different vehicles, different software constraints, different regulators, different capital and different timelines — so they live here, as the first of the adjacent worlds, rather than inside the passenger stack. Autonomous mining and autonomous seafaring (submarines included) are next on my research list, and the delivery-bot world below merits a deep exploration of its own.
